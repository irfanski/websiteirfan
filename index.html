Nike, Inc. is an American shoe, clothing and sports equipment company which is one of the largest in the world.

Most of the factories producing for Nike are located in newly developed areas for light industry in Tangerang and Serang, west of Jakarta. In Korean-owned factories (and some Indonesian-owned ones too) the top management is held by Koreans. Middle level managers and supervisors can also come from Korea or Indonesia.
